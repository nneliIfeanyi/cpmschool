Mission Statement

Giving a child quality education is first step in putting a child in the right track of life. The aim of the school is to ensure a solid foundation in education and moral training in the fear of God. In Glory Land morality is as important as mathematics and English language. Telling children about Jesus and Heaven is one of our major target.
