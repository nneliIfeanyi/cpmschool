A Brief History

The school was kickstarted on 9th of Sept. 2002. Was initially named Glory Land Academy,which started with a boy of 8months old and a little girl, staffed with four male teachers and three female teachers.Thus is our humble beginning which has now given birth to Christ Pentecostal Mission International(CPM) School Suleja ranking among the best in town.

The school was born out of a vision of Mrs Febian Lynda who saw the dire need of a mission school where molding children with christian morals and norms will be one of the aims of the school.

  • Who We Are
  • We are a co-education mission school that welcomes any student in interested in exploring and soaring the fields of academic and moral excellence.
  • Our Vision
  • To ensure a Solid foundation in education and moral training in the fear of God.
  • Our Mission

  • We aim to develop well rounded and thoughtful students prepared to cope with a changing post-modern world and globalized world.

  • Also to raise future leaders with Christ in them who will represent Christ anywhere and change their environment for God to reign.

  • Objectives
  • Preparing students in learning & skillfully developing their intellect through teachers that are breeded in the prensent challenge of learning & global changes.
  • In partnership with families and communities we create relevant learning opportunities for students both inside and outside classroom, which help them develop knowledge and critical thinking skills and character necessary to succed in the globe.
  • We provide student centered educational programs that challenge students to perform in their highest potentials.
  • We collabrate with christian bodies (such as Felloship of Christian Students FCS, and Scrpture Union to ensure sound christian foundation for the pupils/students.
  • We also keep fellowship with the school with other faith based programs to ensure spiritual inpact onthe life of the students.
  • Leadership

Get to know the incredible people that make up our team. Our team is composed of a number of passionate individuals who are committed in playing their role as both mothers and teachers in nuturing and training the young hearts.
